Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I play DotFlee?
The goal is to avoid obstacles and keep your dot safe for as long as possible. Tap and swipe to move the dot and escape the challenges.

2. Is the game free?
Yes, DotFlee is free to play with optional in-app purchases.

Troubleshooting

If the game is not working as expected, try restarting your device or updating the app to the latest version. If the issue persists, contact us for further assistance.
